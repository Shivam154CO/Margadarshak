# Formal Budget Proposal: Evento (Campus Events Management Platform)

## 1. Objective of this Proposal
To request the necessary, minimal budget required to officially launch **Evento**, an internally developed digital platform designed to manage and streamline all campus events, student club activities, and administrative approvals. 

By utilizing modern, cost-effective cloud technologies, we have built a system that replaces expensive third-party software (which typically cost institutions over ₹10 Lakhs to ₹20 Lakhs annually). This document details exactly *how* and *on what* the requested funds will be utilized to ensure the platform is secure, fast, and reliable for all students and staff.

---

## 2. Institutional Continuity (Ensuring the Platform Lasts After We Graduate)

Before requesting funds, it is critical to address how this platform will survive and thrive long after the founding student developers have graduated. We have designed the following continuity plan:

*   **Institutional Ownership:** All server accounts, domain names, and databases will be registered under an official college email address (e.g., `it-admin@ourcollege.edu.in`), not a student's personal email. All payments will be managed directly by the college accounts department.
*   **Move to Dedicated Servers:** We are requesting a "Dedicated Cloud Server (VPS)" (like AWS, DigitalOcean, or Hostinger) rather than temporary starter platforms. A dedicated server acts as a permanent digital real estate for the college that IT staff fully control.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:** We are creating a complete "Handover Manual" containing the entire source code, system architecture, and maintenance instructions. This allows junior computer science students or the college IT Cell to easily take over maintenance as an ongoing capstone project or internship.

## 3. Detailed Breakdown: What Do We Need Money For?

To make the platform accessible to thousands of students 24/7, we must pay for the "digital infrastructure." Below is an explanation of every required component in plain terms:

### A. The Official Web Address (Domain Name)
*   **What it is:** The official name students will type into their browser (e.g., `events.ourcollege.edu.in` or `campusevento.in`).
*   **Why we need it:** It provides a professional and trustworthy destination. Without it, the website is inaccessible to the public.
*   **Cost Estimate:** ~₹800 to ₹1,200 per year.

### B. Dedicated Cloud Server (VPS Hosting)
*   **What it is:** A rented permanent computer in the cloud (Server) that keeps the website online 24/7.
*   **Why we need it:** When a major event opens for registration, thousands of students might log in simultaneously. A proper Virtual Private Server (VPS) ensures the website has enough RAM and processing power to never crash. Because it is a VPS, the college IT cell has full root access to manage it indefinitely.
*   **Cost Estimate:** ~₹1,500/month (₹18,000 per year) for a strong, reliable server (e.g., AWS EC2/Lightsail or DigitalOcean Droplet with 4GB RAM).

### C. Secure Data Storage (Managed Database)
*   **What it is:** A highly secure digital vault where all user accounts, club registrations, event tickets, uploaded flyers, and reports are saved.
*   **Why we need it:** We must store student data securely. A "Managed Database" automatically handles daily backups, so even if a mistake happens, the college never loses historical records of events or student data.
*   **Cost Estimate:** Free for the initial launch phase; approximately ₹20,000/year when scaling to heavily active, daily campus-wide use.

### D. Automated Student Communication (Email API)
*   **What it is:** The automated system that instantly sends email receipts, QR code tickets, and approval notifications.
*   **Why we need it:** Manually emailing students is impossible at scale. We use an enterprise mailing service to guarantee emails land straight in the students' inboxes instantly, avoiding the Spam folder.
*   **Cost Estimate:** ₹0 for up to 3,000 emails/month. ~₹2,500/year for mass emailing (up to 50,000 emails/month).

### E. Advanced "Smart" Features (AI Integration)
*   **What it is:** The artificial intelligence features that automatically generate professional, structured PDF event reports for the principal and administrators.
*   **Why we need it:** This saves hours of administrative time by neatly formatting messy data into clean, printable reports.
*   **Cost Estimate:** Billed "per use." We estimate roughly ₹800 to ₹1,500 per month based on how many reports the college generates.

## 5. Return on Investment (ROI)

If the college were to purchase an equivalent third-party, pre-built software system (such as foreign platforms like *CampusGroups* or *Engage*), the industry standard cost is a **₹10 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs annual licensing fee**. 

Because this application was developed internally by our students specifically for our institution's needs:
1.  **Massive Cost Savings:** The college completely avoids software licensing fees. You only pay for the raw "server electricity" (less than ₹70,000/year even at absolute maximum capacity in Year 3+).
2.  **Data Sovereignty:** The institution retains 100% ownership and legal control over the student data. No third-party corporation has access to our campus analytics.
3.  **Staff Efficiency & Paperless Campus:** Automates manual paper-based approval processes for events, budgets, and post-event reports, saving hundreds of hours of administrative staff time and transitioning the campus completely digital.

## 6. Request for Approval
To permanently migrate the platform to official college servers and launch it for the upcoming semester, we request an initial Year 1 budget allocation of **₹26,000**. This will secure the servers, domain, and AI capabilities required for a successful, stable launch.
